Music Challenge - 2026

The music challenge is back! The music challenge (for lack of a better name) is when I listen through my entire self-hosted music library. The rules are simple:

  • No skipping any songs
  • Remixes, stripped/acoustic versions, extended mixes, and duplicates do not count (unless I want it to)
  • All songs are played in alphabetical order
  • If any music is added during the challenge it still must be rated

... besides that, it's just a few weeks of me playing every song I own! You can browse and search the chart or click an album/artist for detailed information.
